The Best Movies According to Jonathan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Naruto

Naruto is the best anime ever.
What sets it apart from the rest is the way the story is told. Naruto is the story of a boy who becomes a ninja. This story brings out all kinds of emotions, motivations, and laughs. People did not like him because of what he had within him. He did not have friends or family, yet he wanted to get stronger so people would acknowledge him. It's funny that he mastered the "Sexy Jutsu" before anything else. He was brave and a troublemaker. Naruto was not good academically. He fell in love with Sakura, who loved Sasuke. Sasuke was mean and had one goal: to kill his brother, who killed his parents. Naruto took Sasuke and fought him, then took his appearance to talk to Sakura. By the end, Naruto considered Sasuke as his friend. He fought hard and trained hard. When Jiraiya died, it was very emotional. You should watch it. It’s a great movie.
